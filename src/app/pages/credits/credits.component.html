D2H

is a fan app developed by a Dota®2 player, for other Dota 2 players.

D2H is a client app: the only existent server interaction is with OpenDota APIs. D2H will save only on your device your player id, in order to open automatically the Overview page for the default user. D2H won't ask for any private information (such as credentials or personal datas) and won't send any consumed data outside the app.
